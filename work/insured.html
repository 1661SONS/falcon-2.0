Insurers' Continuing Education

For this project, I was part of a small team designing an internal application for insurance professionals. The client needed a way to track and matriculate agents through continuing education courses towards their chartered property casualty underwriter (CPCU) licensing and renewals. Agents would take training courses, attend events or study online to obtain credits towards completion of their requirements. Using Axure, I created wireframes and a responsive interactive prototype for the development of the Corporate Manager (Key Person) system.

Sketches

These sketches represent a collaborative effort with myself and the team's visual designer to provide a simple way for the corporate manager visualize the professional health of their base.

Since the purpose of this system was for the corporate manager to purchase renewals once a CPCU became eligible, we gave that data figure the most visual importance (the #17). If the corporate manager wanted to dive deeper into the data, they could do so by clicking on any of the blue numbers.

This structure was especially conducive for generating quick reports. Clicking on any of the data points would take the corporate manager to a pre-filtered table of all CPCUs in that category. From here, the corporate manager could export the report to a format of their choice.
