Teaching Kids to Type

For this project, the CEO of Typing Club requested a complete overhaul of the teacher, student, and administrative portals. However, given the timeline, we focused just on the teacher role. This required a a major excavation, in that we had to dig deep to the bottom, find what teachers were looking for, and bring it up to the surface.

To prioritize the narrowed scope, we used several card sorting sessions, conducted 8 teacher interviews and sent out a survey. The data gathered from these sources set the tone for our design studio with the client.
